LOL Live Desk

Welcome to your LOL Live Desk application.
This tool is used to retrieve specific data from League of Legends

Launch the game and it will start fetching automatically!

For what this application is used?

This application is designed to retrieve information that is unavailable through the Live Client Data API, such as detailed damage and gold events for in-game statistics.
Without this tool, collecting such statistics would require the use of memory-reading techniques, which are not only prone to errors but could also lead to unintended consequences, including potential bans from League of Legends.
